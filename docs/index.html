This small web app was invented and built by me, Philip White.

I was inspired by DiceKeys and Diceware, but all implementations of Diceware that I could find online were DIY: throw dice, manually look it up in a table. I wanted a one-stop shop for myself, so I built this. I also find randomness fascinating, and building this app was an opportunity to explore it.

This app has the highest respect for your privacy. It generates passphrases entirely offline, and does not use any tracking technologies.

Feel free to get in touch and say hello. This app is developed and hosted on GitHub and licensed under MIT. I welcome your contributions.
